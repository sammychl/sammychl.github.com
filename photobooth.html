About us

We specialize in photobooths and customize them to suit your needs, as well as your budget. Our photobooths can be simple, or it can be fancy and extravagant, with red carpets and custom backdrops.

We offer a variety of choices by having our professional photographers bring a studio-like environment to your venue, complete with customizable backdrops and professional lighting. Monitors and photo printers would also be on site to provide immediate photo reviewing/slideshows, processing and printing . YES, you will get a copy of your photo in under 60 seconds. To bring the most fun to those who come to us, we offer the most extensive collection of props for anyone who wants to have some fun and to take some silly, yet memorable, pictures.
